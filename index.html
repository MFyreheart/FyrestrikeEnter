Welcome to FEN




Hi there and welcome to the "official" website for Fyrestrike Entertainment. As with most things, this whole thing is a bit convoluted and one of us is going to get sick of it eventually, I bet. Anyway, this site is the hub for anything that may pertain to Fyrestrike Entertainment. This means YouTube, Twitch, Discord, etc. But what exactly is Fyrestrike Entertainment?

Fyrestrike Entertainment was originally created as a YouTube Channel called "Bbros999" in 2013, which shifted into "Fyreheart Entertainment" around 2015/2016 with the creation of the Michael Fyreheart character (my name really is Michael though). Some more time passed and the channel became Fyrestrike Entertainment. While most of the branding has changed over time, the name has not changed since.

Since the channel's inception, different types of videos have been posted (some privated due to "reasons"). From stop-motion and a live action "film" to the gaming content that takes up the focus of the channel, Fyrestrike Entertainment has always been a content dump of sorts, but most content does have to meet some standards. In the end, I guess we'll all have to wait and see where this channel goes.
